Pahinga ka, deserve mo 'to!

Comfort Crate is a start-up company founded in 2022. The notion of entrepreneurs and consumers as mutual benefactors inspired the team to come up with potential products that will make self-care worth spending, guilt-free. Driven by the desire to uplift people’s spirits amidst adversities, each set from Comfort Crate was curated for all people in all situations. The group believes that money cannot buy happiness, but it can buy you a crate that can help you stir up your own happiness.
